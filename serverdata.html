JackBlack Rules Goal: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Beat the dealer to win. Card Values: 2–10 = face value J, Q, K = 10 A = 11 or 1 Gameplay: Each player gets 2 cards; dealer gets 2 cards. On your turn, choose: Hit → draw a card Stand → keep your hand If your score > 21 → Busted If your score = 21 → automatic stand Dealer Turn: Dealer draws until reaching 17 or higher. Winning: Highest score ≤ 21 wins. If all players bust → dealer wins. Blackjack (21 on first 2 cards) wins instantly. pretty simple...
